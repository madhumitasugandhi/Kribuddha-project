About Us

At Kribuddha Wellness Center, we believe wellness is the highest form of luxury—a life free from stress, pain, and imbalance. In today’s demanding world where many struggle with anxiety, fatigue, and painful body conditions, we provide personalized consultations along with exclusive team yoga sessions that transform both individuals and groups.

Our approach blends the timeless depth of yoga, meditation, and mindfulness with a premium, holistic touch, ensuring that every participant finds relief, renewal, and inner strength.

Kribuddha is not just a wellness center—it is an ultra-premium sanctuary of healing, team harmony, and self-discovery, where journeys lead to a graceful, elevated lifestyle.

Led by Reetu Varma, our mission is to guide you towards harmony of mind, body & soul.
