What is Yoga Nidra? Benefits and Techniques of Yogic Sleep

By Kribuddha Wellness • July 22, 2025

Yoga Nidra, also known as yogic sleep, is a guided meditation practice where you lie down comfortably and enter a state between sleep and wakefulness. Unlike normal sleep, it keeps the mind aware while the body relaxes deeply.

Benefits of Yoga Nidra

Practicing Yoga Nidra regularly can:

  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Improve sleep quality
  • Boost focus and mental clarity
  • Relieve tension and fatigue

Even 30 minutes of Yoga Nidra can feel as refreshing as 2 hours of deep sleep.

How to Practice (Simple Steps)

Follow these easy steps to begin:

  1. Lie down in Shavasana (corpse pose).
  2. Close your eyes and relax your body.
  3. Follow a guided voice for body scan and breath awareness.
  4. Visualize peaceful images and repeat a positive intention (Sankalpa).

Yoga Nidra is beginner-friendly and can be practiced anytime—morning for energy, evening for relaxation.

If you feel stressed or struggle with sleep, try Yoga Nidra for a calmer, healthier mind and body.
