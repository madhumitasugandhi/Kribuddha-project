Understanding the 7 Chakras: Meanings, Locations, and Benefits

By Kribuddha Wellness • August 14, 2025

Chakras are an important part of ancient Indian philosophy. The word chakra comes from Sanskrit, meaning “wheel” or “disk.” These are energy centers in our body that help control our physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

When chakras are balanced, we feel healthy, calm, and focused. But when they are blocked, we may feel tired, stressed, or emotionally low.

“By concentrating on the chakras, one attains health, longevity, and liberation.”
— Hatha Yoga Pradipika, Chapter 4
